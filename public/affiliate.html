Generate Referral Link

Pick a paid video and copy its referral link. When a buyer uses your link and completes payment, your commission is credited automatically if that video's affiliate program is enabled.

Commission is credited to your wallet after each confirmed purchase.

How It Works
  1. Creators configure commission percentage per video in the Creator Settings tab.
  2. Affiliates copy the referral link for any enabled video.
  3. Buyers purchase through the referral link.
  4. The configured commission is credited automatically after payment is confirmed.
